Dinwiddie County Historic Courthouse

Built in 1851, the *Dinwiddie County Courthouse was the headquarters of Union General Philip H. Sheridan during the Battle of Dinwiddie and the Battle of Five Forks. Markers commemorate battles fought in the County and ten unknown Union soldiers buried in the nearby churchyard.
