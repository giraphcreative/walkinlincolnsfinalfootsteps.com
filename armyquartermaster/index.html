U.S. Army Quartermaster Museum

The U.S. Army Quartermaster Museum is home to significant artifacts including General Grant's saddle used on his favorite horse, Cincinnati. President Lincoln rode Cincinnati into Petersburg on April 3, 1865. An interactive exhibit, "A War of Supply: The Quartermaster Department in the Civil War" is featured. Other Civil War artifacts include Grant's Wagon Ambulance, and extremely rare uniforms. Free admission.
