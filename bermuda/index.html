Bermuda Hundred Campaign

The Bermuda Hundred Campaign was a series of battles fought at the town of Bermuda Hundred. During May 1864 Union Major General Benjamin Butler, commanding the Army of the James, threatened Richmond from the east but was stopped by forces under Confederate General P.G.T. Beauregard. On June 22, 1864, President Lincoln toured the Bermuda Hundred defenses.
