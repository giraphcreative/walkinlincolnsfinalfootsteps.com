Fork Inn

Fork Inn is a restored 14-room tavern built in 1803. It served as a stagecoach stop and was used as a hospital following one of the last Civil War battles. Civil War memorabilia include weapons, personal items, clothing, medical instruments and a Confederate soldier's wooden leg.
